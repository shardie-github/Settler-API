## Hiring Process

### Step 1: Sourcing

**Channels:**
- Job boards (LinkedIn, AngelList, Hacker News)
- Referrals
- Recruiters
- Community (Discord, GitHub)
- Events and conferences

**Sourcing Strategy:**
- Focus on developer communities
- Leverage network and referrals
- Use targeted job boards
- Attend events and conferences

---

### Step 2: Screening

**Resume Review:**
- Technical skills
- Experience level
- Culture fit indicators
- Growth potential

**Phone Screen:**
- 30-minute call
- Technical assessment
- Culture fit assessment
- Role fit assessment

---

### Step 3: Interview

**Interview Process:**
1. **Technical Interview** (60 minutes)
   - Technical skills assessment
   - Problem-solving
   - Code review
   - Architecture discussion

2. **Culture Fit Interview** (45 minutes)
   - Values alignment
   - Team fit
   - Communication
   - Growth mindset

3. **Final Interview** (30 minutes)
   - Founder/CEO interview
   - Role discussion
   - Questions and answers
   - Offer discussion

---

### Step 4: Decision

**Decision Criteria:**
- Technical excellence
- Culture fit
- Growth potential
- Team fit
- Role fit

**Decision Process:**
- Interview feedback review
- Team discussion
- Decision made
- Offer extended

---

### Step 5: Onboarding

**Onboarding Process:**
- Welcome email
- Company overview
- Role-specific training
- Tools and systems access
- First assignments
- Mentorship
