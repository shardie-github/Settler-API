## Async/Remote Rituals

### Daily Standup (Engineering)

**Frequency:** Daily  
**Time:** 9:00 AM PT  
**Duration:** 15 minutes  
**Format:** Async (Slack) or Sync (Video)

**Format:**
- What did you do yesterday?
- What are you doing today?
- Any blockers?

**Tool:** Slack #engineering-standup

---

### Weekly All-Hands

**Frequency:** Weekly  
**Time:** Friday, 2:00 PM PT  
**Duration:** 30 minutes  
**Format:** Video call

**Agenda:**
- Company updates
- Product updates
- Team updates
- Wins and learnings
- Q&A

**Tool:** Zoom

---

### Monthly Team Meeting

**Frequency:** Monthly  
**Time:** First Monday, 10:00 AM PT  
**Duration:** 60 minutes  
**Format:** Video call

**Agenda:**
- Monthly review
- Goals and metrics
- Team updates
- Planning
- Team building

**Tool:** Zoom

---

### Quarterly Planning

**Frequency:** Quarterly  
**Time:** First week of quarter  
**Duration:** Half day  
**Format:** Video call + async

**Agenda:**
- Quarterly review
- Goal setting
- Roadmap planning
- Resource planning
- Team alignment

**Tool:** Notion, Zoom

## Decision Rights

### Product Decisions

**Decision Maker:** Product Manager (or Founder/CEO if no PM)

**Process:**
1. Gather requirements
2. Evaluate options
3. Make decision
4. Communicate decision
5. Implement decision

**Stakeholders:**
- Engineering team
- Design team
- Customer success team
- Customers (for major decisions)

---

### Security Decisions

**Decision Maker:** Security Team (or Founder/CTO)

**Process:**
1. Assess security risk
2. Evaluate options
3. Make decision
4. Implement security measures
5. Document decision

**Stakeholders:**
- Engineering team
- Compliance team
- Legal team

---

### Pricing Decisions

**Decision Maker:** Founder/CEO

**Process:**
1. Analyze market and competition
2. Evaluate pricing options
3. Make decision
4. Communicate to team
5. Update pricing

**Stakeholders:**
- Sales team
- Marketing team
- Finance team
- Customers (for major changes)

---

### Customer-Impact Decisions

**Decision Maker:** Founder/CEO (with input from relevant teams)

**Process:**
1. Assess customer impact
2. Evaluate options
3. Make decision
4. Communicate to customers
5. Implement decision

**Stakeholders:**
- Customer success team
- Support team
- Engineering team
- Customers

## Best Practices

### Communication

**Do's:**
- ✅ Communicate early and often
- ✅ Be clear and concise
- ✅ Use appropriate channels
- ✅ Respond promptly
- ✅ Be respectful

**Don'ts:**
- ❌ Delay communication
- ❌ Use unclear language
- ❌ Over-communicate
- ❌ Ignore messages
- ❌ Be disrespectful

---

### Documentation

**Do's:**
- ✅ Keep documentation updated
- ✅ Use consistent formatting
- ✅ Organize by topic
- ✅ Share knowledge
- ✅ Document decisions

**Don'ts:**
- ❌ Leave documentation outdated
- ❌ Use inconsistent formatting
- ❌ Create duplicate documentation
- ❌ Hoard knowledge
- ❌ Skip documentation

---

### Decision-Making

**Do's:**
- ✅ Gather information
- ✅ Evaluate options
- ✅ Make decisions quickly
- ✅ Communicate decisions
- ✅ Learn from decisions

**Don'ts:**
- ❌ Make decisions without information
- ❌ Delay decisions unnecessarily
- ❌ Make decisions in isolation
- ❌ Skip communication
- ❌ Ignore feedback
