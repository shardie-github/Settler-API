## Exit Strategy

### Strategic Acquisition (Preferred)

**Target Acquirers:**
- Stripe
- Intuit (QuickBooks)
- Shopify
- Fivetran

**Valuation Range:** $50M-$500M  
**Timeline:** 3-5 years  
**Success Criteria:**
- $50M+ ARR
- Strong product-market fit
- Strategic value
- Team expertise

---

### Financial Acquisition

**Target Acquirers:**
- Private equity firms
- Financial buyers
- Roll-up strategies

**Valuation Range:** $20M-$100M  
**Timeline:** 2-4 years  
**Success Criteria:**
- Profitable
- Strong cash flow
- Growth potential
- Market position

---

### IPO (Long-Term)

**Target:** Public markets  
**Valuation Range:** $500M+  
**Timeline:** 7-10 years  
**Success Criteria:**
- $100M+ ARR
- Profitable
- Strong growth
- Market leadership
