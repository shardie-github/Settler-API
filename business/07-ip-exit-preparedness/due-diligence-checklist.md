**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** January 2026

## Due Diligence Overview

**Purpose:** Prepare for acquisition due diligence by documenting what acquirers (Stripe, Intuit, Shopify, Fivetran, BlackLine) would examine.

**Scope:** Legal, code, security, product-market fit, financials, team, operations.

## Due Diligence Timeline

### Typical Timeline

**Week 1-2: Initial Review**
- Document request
- Initial review
- Question list

**Week 3-4: Deep Dive**
- Detailed review
- Follow-up questions
- Site visits

**Week 5-6: Final Review**
- Final questions
- Negotiations
- Closing

**Total:** 6-8 weeks

---

## Due Diligence Best Practices

### Do's

- ✅ Organize documents early
- ✅ Be transparent
- ✅ Respond promptly
- ✅ Provide accurate information
- ✅ Address concerns proactively

### Don'ts

- ❌ Delay responses
- ❌ Hide information
- ❌ Provide inaccurate information
- ❌ Ignore concerns
- ❌ Skip preparation
