## Customer Insight Overview

**Purpose:** Guide customer interviews, beta calls, and surveys to gather actionable insights.

**Use Cases:**
- User interviews
- Beta user calls
- Churn surveys
- Feature requests
- Customer success calls

## User Interview Prompts

### Opening Questions

**1. Tell me about how you're using Settler.**
- Understand current usage
- Identify use cases
- Discover workflows
- Uncover pain points

**2. What problem were you trying to solve when you signed up?**
- Understand initial motivation
- Identify pain points
- Discover use cases
- Validate problem-solution fit

**3. Walk me through your typical workflow.**
- Understand user journey
- Identify friction points
- Discover opportunities
- Validate product-market fit

---

### Discovery Questions

**4. What's working really well for you?**
- Identify strengths
- Discover value drivers
- Understand success factors
- Validate value proposition

**5. What's frustrating or not working?**
- Identify pain points
- Discover friction points
- Understand blockers
- Validate improvement opportunities

**6. What features do you wish existed?**
- Discover feature requests
- Identify gaps
- Understand needs
- Validate roadmap

**7. What would make Settler 10x more valuable?**
- Identify high-impact opportunities
- Discover game-changing features
- Understand vision
- Validate strategic direction

---

### Validation Questions

**8. How does Settler compare to [competitor]?**
- Understand competitive positioning
- Identify differentiators
- Discover advantages
- Validate competitive moat

**9. What would make you recommend Settler to a colleague?**
- Identify referral drivers
- Discover advocacy factors
- Understand value proposition
- Validate product-market fit

**10. What would make you upgrade to a higher tier?**
- Identify upgrade drivers
- Discover expansion opportunities
- Understand value perception
- Validate pricing strategy

---

### Closing Questions

**11. Is there anything else you'd like to share?**
- Open-ended opportunity
- Discover additional insights
- Capture feedback
- Build relationship

**12. Can we follow up in a few weeks to see how things are going?**
- Build relationship
- Schedule follow-up
- Maintain engagement
- Track progress

## Best Practices

### Do's

- ✅ Ask open-ended questions
- ✅ Listen actively
- ✅ Follow up on interesting responses
- ✅ Thank participants
- ✅ Document insights

### Don'ts

- ❌ Ask leading questions
- ❌ Interrupt participants
- ❌ Skip follow-up questions
- ❌ Forget to thank participants
- ❌ Ignore insights
