## Activation Metrics

### Time to First Value

**Definition:** Time from signup to first successful reconciliation.

**Target:** <24 hours  
**Measurement:** Analytics dashboard  
**Tracking:** Daily

**Why It Matters:**
- Faster activation = higher conversion
- Indicates product ease of use
- Predicts long-term engagement

---

### Activation Rate

**Definition:** % of users who create first job within 7 days.

**Target:** 60%+  
**Measurement:** Analytics dashboard  
**Tracking:** Weekly

**Why It Matters:**
- Higher activation = better product-market fit
- Indicates product value
- Predicts retention

---

### Engagement Rate

**Definition:** % of users who run reconciliations weekly.

**Target:** 80%+  
**Measurement:** Analytics dashboard  
**Tracking:** Weekly

**Why It Matters:**
- Higher engagement = better retention
- Indicates product value
- Predicts expansion

---

## Retention Metrics

### Monthly Churn Rate

**Definition:** % of customers who cancel per month.

**Target:** <5%  
**Measurement:** Analytics dashboard  
**Tracking:** Monthly

**Why It Matters:**
- Lower churn = better product-market fit
- Indicates product value
- Predicts long-term success

---

### Annual Churn Rate

**Definition:** % of customers who cancel per year.

**Target:** <30%  
**Measurement:** Analytics dashboard  
**Tracking:** Annually

**Why It Matters:**
- Lower churn = better retention
- Indicates product value
- Predicts LTV

---

### Retention Rate

**Definition:** % of customers retained after X months.

**Target:** 80%+ after 6 months  
**Measurement:** Cohort analysis  
**Tracking:** Monthly

**Why It Matters:**
- Higher retention = better product-market fit
- Indicates product value
- Predicts growth

---

## Expansion Metrics

### Expansion Revenue

**Definition:** Revenue from existing customers (upgrades, add-ons).

**Target:** 20% of MRR  
**Measurement:** Analytics dashboard  
**Tracking:** Monthly

**Why It Matters:**
- Higher expansion = better growth
- Indicates product value
- Predicts LTV

---

### Upgrade Rate

**Definition:** % of customers who upgrade to higher tier within 6 months.

**Target:** 15%+  
**Measurement:** Analytics dashboard  
**Tracking:** Monthly

**Why It Matters:**
- Higher upgrade = better product value
- Indicates growth potential
- Predicts revenue growth

---

### Add-On Revenue

**Definition:** Revenue from add-ons (custom adapters, log retention).

**Target:** 10% of MRR  
**Measurement:** Analytics dashboard  
**Tracking:** Monthly

**Why It Matters:**
- Higher add-on = better monetization
- Indicates product value
- Predicts revenue growth

---

## Referral Metrics

### Referral Rate

**Definition:** % of customers who refer others.

**Target:** 20%+  
**Measurement:** Referral system  
**Tracking:** Monthly

**Why It Matters:**
- Higher referral = better product-market fit
- Indicates customer satisfaction
- Predicts organic growth

---

### Referral Conversion Rate

**Definition:** % of referrals who become customers.

**Target:** 30%+  
**Measurement:** Referral system  
**Tracking:** Monthly

**Why It Matters:**
- Higher conversion = better referrals
- Indicates product value
- Predicts growth

---

### Viral Coefficient

**Definition:** Number of new customers per existing customer.

**Target:** >1.0  
**Measurement:** Referral system  
**Tracking:** Monthly

**Why It Matters:**
- >1.0 = viral growth
- Indicates product-market fit
- Predicts exponential growth

## Product-Market Fit Indicators

### Net Promoter Score (NPS)

**Definition:** % of promoters minus % of detractors.

**Target:** >50  
**Measurement:** Quarterly survey  
**Tracking:** Quarterly

**Why It Matters:**
- Higher NPS = better product-market fit
- Indicates customer satisfaction
- Predicts growth

---

### Customer Satisfaction (CSAT)

**Definition:** Average satisfaction score (1-5).

**Target:** 4.5/5.0  
**Measurement:** Post-interaction survey  
**Tracking:** Weekly

**Why It Matters:**
- Higher CSAT = better experience
- Indicates customer satisfaction
- Predicts retention

---

### Product-Market Fit Score

**Definition:** % of users who would be "very disappointed" if product disappeared.

**Target:** 40%+  
**Measurement:** Survey  
**Tracking:** Quarterly

**Why It Matters:**
- Higher score = better product-market fit
- Indicates product value
- Predicts success

---

## Growth Metrics

### Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)

**Definition:** Total monthly recurring revenue.

**Target:** $50K (Month 12)  
**Measurement:** Analytics dashboard  
**Tracking:** Monthly

**Why It Matters:**
- Higher MRR = better growth
- Indicates business health
- Predicts success

---

### Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)

**Definition:** Total annual recurring revenue.

**Target:** $600K (Year 1)  
**Measurement:** Analytics dashboard  
**Tracking:** Annually

**Why It Matters:**
- Higher ARR = better growth
- Indicates business health
- Predicts success

---

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

**Definition:** Cost to acquire one customer.

**Target:** <$300  
**Measurement:** Analytics dashboard  
**Tracking:** Monthly

**Why It Matters:**
- Lower CAC = better efficiency
- Indicates marketing effectiveness
- Predicts profitability

---

### Lifetime Value (LTV)

**Definition:** Total revenue from customer over lifetime.

**Target:** $3,600  
**Measurement:** Analytics dashboard  
**Tracking:** Monthly

**Why It Matters:**
- Higher LTV = better value
- Indicates product value
- Predicts profitability

---

### LTV:CAC Ratio

**Definition:** Lifetime value divided by customer acquisition cost.

**Target:** >10x  
**Measurement:** Analytics dashboard  
**Tracking:** Monthly

**Why It Matters:**
- Higher ratio = better unit economics
- Indicates profitability
- Predicts success

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Top 10 KPIs

1. **Activation Rate** (60%+)
2. **Monthly Churn Rate** (<5%)
3. **NPS** (>50)
4. **MRR Growth** (20%+ MoM)
5. **LTV:CAC Ratio** (>10x)
6. **Engagement Rate** (80%+)
7. **Expansion Revenue** (20% of MRR)
8. **Referral Rate** (20%+)
9. **Time to First Value** (<24 hours)
10. **Product-Market Fit Score** (40%+)
