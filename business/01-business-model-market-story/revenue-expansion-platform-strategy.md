**Version:** 1.0

## Executive Summary

Settler's revenue strategy is built on multiple monetization layers that create compounding value as customers grow. Beyond core reconciliation, we monetize through AI features, premium extensions, marketplace ecosystem, and embedded/white-label offerings.

**Revenue Philosophy:**
- **Start Simple:** Core reconciliation at affordable price
- **Grow Together:** Revenue scales with customer success
- **Expand Value:** Multiple revenue streams create sustainable growth
- **Platform Play:** Marketplace and ecosystem create network effects

## Partner Onboarding Process

### Step 1: Partner Application

**Application Form:**
- Company information
- Integration type
- Customer base size
- Strategic alignment
- Expected volume

**Review Process:**
- Technical review (1 week)
- Business review (1 week)
- Approval/Rejection (2 weeks total)

### Step 2: Technical Integration

**Integration Steps:**
1. **API Access:** Provide partner API keys
2. **Documentation:** Share integration docs
3. **Testing:** Test integration in sandbox
4. **Production:** Deploy to production

**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks

### Step 3: Go-to-Market

**Co-Marketing Activities:**
- Joint blog posts
- Co-hosted webinars
- Case studies
- Product announcements

**Timeline:** Ongoing
