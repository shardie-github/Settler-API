# Go-to-Market Strategy

**Last Updated:** January 2026

---

## GTM Overview

Settler's go-to-market strategy combines **product-led growth (PLG)** with **developer-led growth** and **sales-assisted motion** for enterprise customers. We target mid-market e-commerce and SaaS companies through multiple channels, with a focus on developer experience and self-service onboarding.

---

## GTM Phases

### Phase 1: Developer-Led Growth (Months 1-6)
**Goal:** 1,000 beta users → 100 paying customers → $5K MRR

**Strategy:**
- Product Hunt launch
- Technical blog posts and content marketing
- Developer community engagement (Twitter/X, Hacker News, Indie Hackers)
- Partnerships (Stripe Partner Program, Shopify App Store)
- Free tier (1K reconciliations/month)

**Channels:**
1. **Product Hunt Launch** (Month 4)
   - Target: Top 5 Product of the Day
   - Preparation: Landing page, demo video, early supporters
   - Follow-up: Blog post, social media, email to signups

2. **Technical Blog Posts** (Ongoing)
   - "How we built Settler" series
   - "Reconciliation best practices" guides
   - "API integration tutorials"
   - SEO-optimized content targeting "Stripe Shopify reconciliation", "QuickBooks API integration"

3. **Developer Communities** (Ongoing)
   - Hacker News: Share technical blog posts, answer questions
   - Indie Hackers: Share journey, get feedback
   - Reddit (r/SaaS, r/ecommerce, r/stripe): Answer questions, share insights
   - Twitter/X: Engage with developers, share updates

4. **Partnerships** (Months 3-6)
   - Stripe Partner Directory: List Settler as reconciliation partner
   - Shopify App Store: Build Shopify app for order reconciliation
   - QuickBooks App Store: Build QuickBooks integration
   - API Directories: RapidAPI, Postman, API List

**Success Metrics:**
- 1,000 beta users
- 100 paying customers
- $5K MRR
- 10% conversion rate (free → paid)
- NPS >50

   - Contract negotiation

5. **Onboarding** (Customer Success)

   - Training sessions
   - Go-live support

## Pricing Strategy

### Free Tier Strategy
**Purpose:**
- Product-led growth (try before you buy)
- Viral loops (free users become advocates)
- Market education (teach developers about reconciliation)
- Competitive moat (hard for competitors to match)

**Free Tier Limits:**
- 1,000 reconciliations/month
- 2 adapters
- 7-day log retention
- Community support

**Conversion Target:**
- 5-10% conversion to paid within 6 months
- Average time to convert: 3-4 months
- Average first paid plan: Starter ($29/month)

---

### Paid Tier Strategy
**Tiers:**
- **Starter**: $29/month (10K reconciliations)
- **Growth**: $99/month (100K reconciliations)
- **Scale**: $299/month (1M reconciliations)
- **Enterprise**: Custom (unlimited)

**Pricing Philosophy:**
- Transparent, usage-based pricing
- No hidden fees
- Upgrade as you scale
- Overage pricing: $0.01 per reconciliation

**Value Proposition:**
- Starter: Small e-commerce stores, early-stage SaaS
- Growth: Mid-market SaaS, growing e-commerce
- Scale: Large e-commerce, enterprise SaaS
- Enterprise: Large enterprises, regulated industries
